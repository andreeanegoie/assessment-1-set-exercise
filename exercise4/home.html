Materials and print quality

The aim is to give you a print that looks rich in colour and lasts for many years.

  • Canvas: 100% cotton canvas with a smooth, matte finish.
  • Frame: Sturdy, kiln-dried wooden stretcher bars.
  • Inks: Pigment-based, fade-resistant inks designed for long-lasting prints.
  • Finish: Printed edge-to-edge, with the image wrapped around the sides of the frame.

Each canvas is checked for colour, sharpness, and marks before it leaves the studio, so you receive a clean and ready-to-hang piece of wall art.

Packaging and shipping

  • The canvas is wrapped in a protective sleeve to prevent scratches and dust.
  • Corner protectors are added to help avoid bumps during transport.
  • The wrapped canvas is placed in a strong cardboard box sized to fit the frame.
