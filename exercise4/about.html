About me

My name is Andreea. I study Computing at Bath Spa University and work part time as a Vendor Manager for a translation company based in Epsom. I live in London with my partner, and my family is in Romania, so travel is a big part of my life. I enjoy visiting new places and taking photos that remind me of the moments I want to keep. This site brings together some of the views and experiences that stayed with me while travelling.
